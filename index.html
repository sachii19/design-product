ABOUT


LapEl is a height elevator for your laptop.
Finger and hand pains is an issue which is caused
by excess amount of typing and it has significantly
gone higher since the start of Covid-19.
The height is adjustabe and can be set to your preference.
Now you can type and not worry about any pain with LapEl!

FEATURES


Not only can LapEl relieve you from finger and hand pain,it is
safe for your laptop with edges that will not allow it to fall.
LapEl can be adjusted to three heights and when the platform
is required to be flat, the detachable stand can be stuck to the magnet.
All the materials used in LapEl are recyclable and
reusable, whcih makes it eco-friendly!
